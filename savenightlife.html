It's Official

NTIA’s Savenightlife CIC and Lady of the House have teamed up to support women who live, work, and play within the nighttime industry. The partnership follows the three pillars to celebrate, champion and honour women by raising awareness of the challenges faced by them.

Savenightlife & Lady of the House work together to create a continuous blueprint for our community, ensuring women’s visibility, legacy, and space are respected, protected, and valued.

The partnership kicked off with a Safety of Women in the Night-Time Industry survey launched to dig deeper into the experiences of women in the industry.
