It's Official

NTIA’s Savenightlife CIC and Lady of the House have teamed up to support women who live, work, and play within the nighttime industry. The partnership follows the three pillars to celebrate, champion and honour women by raising awareness of the challenges faced by them.NTIA’s Savenightlife CIC and Lady of the House are teaming up to support women who live, work, and play within the night time industry. The partnership will follow the three pillars to celebrate, champion and honour women with a goal to raise awareness of the challenges faced by them.

Savenightlife & Lady of the House work together to create a continuous blueprint for our community, ensuring women’s visibility, legacy, and space are respected, protected, and valued.

The partnership kicked off with a Safety of Women in the Night-Time Industry survey launched to dig deeper into the experiences of women in the industry. You can read the findings below in the public report which has been used as an evidence base for further action within the partnership. Read the survey now:

The partnership will follow the three pillars created by The Lady of The House to celebrate, champion and honour women in the night time industry with joint initiatives including;

  • Celebrate

    • To award women in the industry for their excellent work on a regional level by recognising their merits.

  • Champion

    • To elevate women with coaching, mentoring and education through workshops, webinars and networking events.

  • Honour

    • Creating sector respect of women by protecting their safety and wellbeing across the sector whilst upholding the legacy of women's pivotal role within the industry.

    Silvana Kill, Director of Operations, NTIA and Savenightlife CIC said:

    “Lady of the House has helped us to bring the music industry’s answer to systematic change, with our newfound partnership - 'Savenightlife x Lady of the House'. Our goal is to mentor, award and prioritise the safety of women within the industry.”

    "The challenges faced by women as they navigate their careers is a key focus for Savenightlife , where the aim is to empower individuals and encourage them to use their skills to reach their full potential. To do this, it's imperative we engage with women across every sector in order to learn about their experiences on the ground, and offer solutions to the barriers and biases too frequently faced.”

    "In depth engagement and input from everyone is critical to our Call to Action mission, and armed with that knowledge, our partnership aims to steer towards positive cultural change. By removing the opportunity for the embedding of subconscious biases, cultures begin to form that are solely based on respect, productivity and high performance, from the ground up all the way to the board, creating a safe, dynamic and culturally diverse workplace that benefits all."

    Laila McKenzie, Founder of Lady of the House & Co-Author of the book said: “We are creating a continuous blueprint for our community to ensure that women’s visibility, legacy, and space is respected, protected and valued. Working with Silvana Kill and Savenightlife will enable this to be rolled out into the Night-Time Industry with a lasting impact.”
