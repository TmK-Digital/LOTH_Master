Who we are


We are a female-led organisation with 25 years combined experience in the music and night time industries. Our team is extremely eager to empower women and ensure equality/diversity across the board

What we do


We champion, celebrate and honor women in the music industry by promoting diversity and inclusion in all different fields.

Why do we do it?


As women who have worked at all levels of the music industry, we are fully aware of the gender disparities, inequalities and perpetual abuse that women have, and continue to face in our industry. We want empower women and educate our male counterparts to promote healthier workplaces, better conduct and leave no room for.

Our Values


This is a platform for the women that have continued to uphold the music scene but long been overlooked. Most importantly this is a blueprint for future generations of women to turn to for inspiration, hope and empowerment for House music all across the board.

Our Vision
